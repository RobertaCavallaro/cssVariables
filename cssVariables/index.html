Avatar Movie

My favorite Movie

It is my favorite movie because it has lots of action and amazing graphic effects. It has stunning 3d technoloy. It has an high resolution photography and it is an epic science fiction.

Favorite character

My favorite character is Neytiri, because she is a brave and strong woman ready to sacrifice herself for her family. She has a pure heart and real warrior against the enemies.

Story behind

To explore Pandora, genetically matched human scientists use Na'vi-human hybrids called "avatars." Paraplegic Marine Jake Sully is sent to Pandora to replace his deceased identical twin, who had signed up to be an operator.
